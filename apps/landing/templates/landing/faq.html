Your Frequently Asked Questions answered.

Q1: What is Aptitude Assessment?

Aptitude Assessment is a test to reveal the competency of an individual to do a certain kind of activity. The result is based on inborn facts, which is what we are born with naturally. It does not presume things that have been learnt or trained for, nor is it an attitude test. Each person only needs to do the test once as fingerprints do not change throughout one’s life.

Q2: Is this some kind of fortune telling?

No, we base the assessment on fingerprints which do not change, unlike the lines of palmistry that changes throughout life. We do not predict what the future holds, rather we consult on one’s inborn character and talents.

Q3: Why do I need this assessment for my children?

Each child is unique, they have their own personality and talents. The purpose of this assessment is to assist parents to understand more of their child’s mental and intellectual make up. When we understand the uniqueness of each child we can tailor the development path to suit the child’s talents. In the end children will be the ones with life-long benefits as they are guided by knowledgeable and informed parents.

Q4: What can I find out from the assessment?

There are 3 major factors that are revealed:

  1. Inborn character

    By knowing your child’s personality, the assessment will recommend effective methods to handle and interact with your child. Parents will have a much clearer idea on how to communicate with a child at a minimal conflict level based on their inborn character and communication style.

  2. Congenital learning style

    Find out which education method best suits your child. Parents can teach their child effectively and efficiently, less effort and more result.

  3. Innate talent

    At Aptitude World we believe that every child has a unique potential. We aim to help parents uncover their child’s talents to ensure there is no lost opportunity and that there is awareness of what mother nature has given to their child. It is finding all these factors that better equip parents in their journey of guiding their children, either in academic, extra-curricular activities or in their daily communication and relationship with the child.

Q5: How accurate is the assessment?

We have >95% satisfaction rate and have had numerous trials on adults (which are considered as finished product) to ensure the accuracy of the assessment. We use decades worth of statistics worldwide which is continuously updated to ensure our product stability.

Q6: Where does the assessment take place?

The advantage of having technology is that we can bring it with us anywhere. We can meet you at your child’s school/preschool or a shopping centre or at the comfort of your home if you prefer.

Q8: What happens to the biometric data after the process is completed?

We do not store biometric data permanently. As soon as the report is produced our software is programmed to delete the data from the system. This is to protect the privacy of each client and is part of our policy.

Q9: I lost the report, how can I get it back?
